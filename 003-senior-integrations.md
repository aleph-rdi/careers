# Senior Integrations Engineer (003)

**Location:** Remote (North America preferred)

## About the Role

We're looking for a Senior Integrations Engineer to own the connective tissue between our data platform and the healthcare systems our customers depend on. You'll work across a landscape of EMR APIs, data feeds, and legacy formats—building reliable pipelines that normalize messy clinical data into our unified platform.

## What You'll Do

* Build and maintain integrations with EHR/EMR systems (PointClickCare, SigmaCare, Epic, and others)
* Design ingestion pipelines that normalize heterogeneous clinical data into our unified data model
* Navigate incomplete or inconsistent API documentation, data feeds, and authentication flows to build working integrations
* Own data quality—validate, reconcile, and monitor incoming data across facility integrations
* Collaborate with backend engineers on schema design for healthcare entities (patients, encounters, assessments, orders)
* Build and maintain FHIR-compatible interfaces as the industry transitions from legacy formats
* Debug data discrepancies across systems and trace issues to their source
* Document integration patterns, API quirks, and institutional knowledge that keeps the team moving fast

## What You Bring

* 5+ years building integrations against third-party APIs, including poorly documented or inconsistent ones
* Strong software engineering fundamentals—you write production-grade code, not scripts held together with tape
* Deep experience with relational databases—schema design, complex queries, data modeling
* Comfort working with REST APIs, webhooks, SFTP feeds, and diverse data transport mechanisms
* Familiarity with healthcare data formats: HL7v2, FHIR, CCD/CCDA, or similar
* Experience with data normalization—mapping inconsistent source data into clean, canonical models
* Security-minded: you understand authentication flows, session management, and secure API design

## Nice to Have

* Direct experience with skilled nursing facility / long-term care software (PointClickCare, MatrixCare/ResMed, SigmaCare)
* Understanding of SNF clinical workflows: MDS, PDPM, progress notes, physician orders, change-in-condition protocols
* Experience with healthcare compliance requirements (HIPAA, BAAs, data governance)
* Familiarity with Golang, gRPC/Protobuf, or our broader stack
* Background in data relay / flat-file feed processing from PCC or similar EMRs

## Who You Are

* Resourceful and persistent—you find a path forward when documentation is sparse and APIs are quirky
* Fast executor who ships working integrations, not proposals about integrations
* Excellent async communicator—clear, concise, written-first
* Comfortable with ambiguity; you know how to get things to "done"
* High agency—you take ownership and drive outcomes
* Genuinely curious about the healthcare domain, not just the technical puzzles
